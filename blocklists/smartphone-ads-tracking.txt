# Smartphone DNS blocking lists by ftpihole - https://www.ftpihole.com/

# Designed for and tested on the Pi Hole but should work on all DNS or HOSTS or list of domains based filters including AdGuard Home or Android apps such as AdAway, DNS66, etc...

# As the name suggests this file is and will be focused on blocking ads, analytics, location guessing scripts, and other nasties from smartphones and smartphone apps. Also useful for tablets.
# Will include entries for iOS and Android as well as apps which are platform agnostic.

# The aim with this list is to keep things working correctly while still reducing ads and trackers as much as possible.
# This is the safe list. Designed specifically so that you could, if you wanted, set it up for your grandma and it'd work fine without her having to know what "one of these new fangled Pie Holes" even is.

# Further "risky" lists that block more but could break things, or lists that are explicitly designed to block certain sites (looking your way Zuck), are on ftpihole.com with more coming soon if you are feeling wild.

# Credit: most of this list comes from me scanning my own Pi Hole logs, some comes from other lists I see around the net.
# Thank you to everyone else who makes these lists, contributes to the Pi Hole and similar project efforts, and ultimately makes all this possible.
# But especially me because I'm brilliant ;)
